I don’t sell your data. I only share personal details if:

5) Cookies and local storage

This site does not set cookies. Some pages may use client-side storage (e.g., your filter choices) to remember simple preferences on your device only.

6) Third-party services

To deliver fonts/icons quickly, pages may load resources from:

These providers may receive your IP address and user agent when your browser requests those files. You can block third-party resources at the browser level; the site will still work (with fallback fonts/icons).

If you’ve shared personal data with me, you can request to access, correct, or delete it by emailing me. I aim to respect global privacy principles (e.g., GDPR) and South Africa’s POPIA in good faith as an individual developer.
